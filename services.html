Chidebru Techs Services

Chidebru Techs offers varied web designs and web enhancements as elaborated below.

ui design

Chidebru designs a website that properly represents your company, and makes it easy to use for all web visitors by taking into consideration the appearance, layout, and contents of the site.

ux design

Chidebru delivers products that look both nice and right making the experience of using the products as enjoyable as possible. We personalize products to user’s needs, and deliver functionality in a predictable way.

Responsive Web Design

Screen sizes are always changing, so it's important that a site can adapt to any screen size, today or in the future. Chidebru designs website contents that move dynamically depending on screen size to make it look good on all devices (desktops, tablets, and phones etc).

Online Store Design

Do you own a business? Are you limited with territorial restrictions? Struggle no more. Chidebru designs and develops web stores with high converting themes tailored towards boosting your business globally.

Landing Page

Chidebru designs landing pages with clear call-to-action (CTA) for increasing the conversion rates of an Online Store. It’s where a visitor “lands” first after clicking on a link in an ad from say Google, Bing, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, email or similar places on the web.

Web Maintenance

Just like health can fall apart if it goes too long without a regular check up, so can the health of a website be. Website maintenance is the act of regularly checking the website for issues and mistakes then keeping it updated and relevant. This encourages continued traffic growth, and strengthens SEO and Google rankings.

Web Management

Maintenance involves posting new words and images on the website whenever the company needs to update the information on the site. Chidebru Techs does the updates or where need be encourages the clients who wish to do it by themselves to use CMS as the alternative.

Content Management System

CMS helps clients update their websites without the need to write all the source-codes from scratch (or even know how to code at all). Chidebru Techs specializes in WordPress CMS, and use it sparingly based on the customer requirement assessment.

Blog Pages

Blogs are presented in reverse chronological order (newer contents appear first). and can be part of a larger website. Often businesses do have a blog section where they regularly create content to inform and educate their customers. Use of Blogs helps improve Google SEO rankings.
