About Chidebru Techs

Chimoyi Deusdarius Brunoh, born and raised in Western Kenya. It's out of the first letters of the names that the name Chidebru the Brand Name emanates from. Techs is short for various technologies. Undoubtedly, technology is running this World and the future is becoming more technologically sophisticated. My Software Engineering journey began back in the year 2018, aroused by my passion for Computer Science (specially Software Engineering). Chidebru loves taking up challenges, practicing problem-solving skills and being able to think algorithmically (logically).

Turning a new leaf of life wasn't easy since I had exhausted the available resources in Higher Education for another course in Economics. I could'nt enroll for another professional course immediately due to the financial constraints. At the same time I didn't know anybody in the Software Engineering field to kinda guide me through my passion. I had to do my due diligence to determine the starting point. That's when I resorted to be a self-taught developer. Thanks for the internet of things. I read relevant books and documentations, watched video tutorials, took online courses like W3schools, SoloLearn, and finally enrolled for the Edx CS50x classes. I consulted some of the few close developers I bumped on along the way and followed other senior developers in github to be as competent as possible until I built my brand Chidebru Techs. My journey continues...
